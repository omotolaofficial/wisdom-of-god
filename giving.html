Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes! RCCG Wisdom of Parish is a registered non-profit organization. You'll receive a tax-deductible receipt for every donation made. Keep your receipt for tax purposes.
Is my personal information secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal and financial information. Your privacy is our top priority.
Can I set up recurring giving?
Yes! You can set up monthly, quarterly, or annual recurring donations. This helps us plan ministry programs and makes giving convenient for you.
What if I want to give anonymously?
You can leave the name and email fields blank during checkout. Your donation will be recorded, but you'll remain anonymous in our records.
Can I donate on behalf of someone?
Yes! You can make a donation for a family member, friend, or in honor of someone. Simply include their name in the "Your Name" field or add a note describing the gift.
How are my donations used?
Your donations support our ministry programs, community outreach, staff, facility maintenance, worship materials, and mission work. We maintain transparency in how we use funds and are happy to share detailed reports upon request.
