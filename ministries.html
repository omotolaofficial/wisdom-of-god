Our Ministries

Serving together in love, spreading the Gospel, and building a stronger church community

Children Ministry

We believe that children are a blessing from God. Our Children Ministry is dedicated to nurturing young minds in faith, creating a safe and joyful environment where children can learn about Jesus, develop spiritually, and make lifelong friendships.

Service Times
Sundays: 9:30 AM (with special sunday school)
Age Group
3 - 11 years old
Location
Children's Wing, Main Sanctuary
Led by: Sister Amaka Nwosu

Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry empowers young adults (12-25 years) to grow in their faith, discover their purpose, and make a positive impact in their communities. We provide fellowship, mentorship, and opportunities for spiritual growth.

Meeting Times
Saturdays: 3:00 PM - 5:30 PM
Age Group
12 - 25 years old
Location
Youth Hall, Fellowship Centre
Led by: Pastor Musa Adeyemi

Men Fellowship

Men Fellowship is a brotherhood built on faith, accountability, and purpose. We come together to support one another, strengthen our families, and be the spiritual leaders God has called us to be in our homes and communities.

Meeting Times
Fridays: 6:30 PM (Fellowship & Bible Study)
Who Can Join
All men 18 years and above
Location
Main Sanctuary, Annex Building
Led by: Deacon David Okafor

Women Fellowship

Our Women Fellowship is a circle of love and support. We empower women to embrace their faith, develop their gifts, care for their families, and serve with passion. Together, we grow stronger in Christ.

Meeting Times
Thursdays: 3:00 PM (Fellowship & Prayer)
Who Can Join
All women 18 years and above
Location
Women's Centre, Fellowship Wing
Led by: Mother Blessing Okonkwo

Choir Ministry

Our Choir lifts praises to God through music and song. Whether you're a vocalist, instrumentalist, or simply love worshipping through music, we welcome you to join our harmonious family and enhance our worship experience.

Rehearsal Times
Wednesdays: 6:00 PM, Sundays: 8:00 AM
Requirements
Passion for worship and singing
Location
Music Room, Main Sanctuary
Led by: Brother Chukwu Eze (Choir Master)
