Pastor's Welcome

Welcome to Our Church Family.

Dear Beloved in Christ,

Grace and peace to you in the name of our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ. On behalf of our entire congregation, I joyfully welcome you to our church. Whether you are a longtime member, a first-time visitor, or simply exploring, we are delighted that you are here.

My prayer is that this space will be more than just a site; it will be a doorway into fellowship, encouragement, and spiritual growth. Here you will find resources to strengthen your faith, opportunities to connect with others, and updates on how God is moving in our midst.

We believe that the church is not just a building, but a family united in love, worship, and service. As you browse, may you sense the warmth of our community and the presence of the Holy Spirit guiding you.

We invite you to join us in worship, in prayer, and in service, as together we seek to glorify God and share His love with the world.

Welcome, and may the Lord bless you richly. Yours in Christ,
Pastor Kunle Kunbi
